About The Band

Iksa Nitha is a multilingual fusion band from Karnataka known for combining the energy of rock, the soul of Indian classical, and the elegance of contemporary music.

Performing in Kannada, Hindi the band creates unforgettable experiences through powerful vocals, rich harmonies, and dynamic stage presence.
